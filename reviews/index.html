What Our Guests & Critics Say

Over the years, Vatan has been honored with reviews and nominations from top food critics, publications, and our wonderful local diners. Read their quotes below.

"This is novel Indian cuisine."
New York Magazine
"Instead of just stepping over the border into the exotic, the nineteen-dish meal (all-you-can-eat) celebrates a vegetarian culinary culture still unusual to America."
The New Yorker
"In almost every way, Vatan is an astounding restaurant. From the outside, the windowless facade looks plain. But enter through the hallway with polished bamboo walls and you are transported to a bright, animated Indian village. [...] A complete meal is served in Gujarati fashion, consisting of a dozen or more dishes, most flavored with ghee, an Indian form of clarified butter, and garam masala, a mixture of spices."
The New York Times
Reviewer: Eric Asimov
"Vatan will leave you in a well-fed stupor worth the wake-up call."
Newsday
"Vatan is more than a Gujarati restaurant; it is an experience... gives diners a vacation from New York."
India in New York
An "incredible variety of flavors" from "delicate" to "spicy" emerges from the kitchen of this Murray Hill vegetarian Indian where the "authentic Gujarati" specialties come in an all-you-can-eat format [...] a transporting "village" setting completes the "totally unique experience."
Zagat 2017
The Definitive Verdict
"Windowless Vatan turns its back on a drab stretch of Third Avenue; a long walk down a bamboo hall completes your geographic dislocation to an Indian village, where you find yourself standing in the central square beneath a spreading banyan tree... You don't need a menu—there's one set meal..."
The Village Voice
Reviewer: Robert Sietsema
"...Vatan, the East Indian oasis. You get to eat as much as you want of everything on the menu. An added bennie: there's no shlepping back and forth to the buffet, no endless passing of platters—each diner gets a huge tray thali, holding what seems like dozens of little bowls of vegetable-spice combinations and recombinations. How very, very Gujarati."
New York Press
The Dining Experience
"Vatan is also a user favorite on Yelp.com and CitySearch.com. Repeatedly nominated for City's Best."
User Testimonials
