VATAN CELEBRATES THE FLAVORS OF GUJARAT AND THE AMBIANCE OF A TYPICAL INDIAN COURTYARD

Welcome to Vatan Indian Vegetarian, a Murray Hill and Kips Bay culinary landmark in Midtown Manhattan. For years, we have transported our guests from the hustle and bustle of New York City straight into the heart of a rustic, charming village courtyard in Gujarat, India.

Our dining room is carefully crafted with thatched roofs, hand-painted tables, visual village dioramas, and an iconic 20-foot tall banyan tree center-stage. This immersive setting provides a warm, magical atmosphere that is perfect for romantic dinners, family gatherings, corporate bookings, and special celebrations.

We specialize in the traditional Gujarati Thali—a prix fixe, all-you-can-eat table-side buffet consisting of distinct courses. It is a royal feast designed to let you experience the full breadth of Western Indian flavors, ranging from subtle and sweet to rich and fiery. We operate with strict dietary care, offering dedicated Jain, Swaminarayan, Vegan, Gluten-Free, and Nut-Free thalis.

LET US TRANSPORT YOU TO INDIA WHILE YOU ENJOY OUR HIGHLY ACCLAIMED PRIX FIXE THALI

Our signature meal is served in a course format featuring fresh, high-quality vegetarian ingredients. Our appetizers include classics like Samosas, Sev Puri, Muthia, and Ragda Patis. For the entree, you will receive our Toor Dal, potato curries, Bhaji (spinach and corn), Chole (chickpeas), and seasonal vegetables, accompanied by steamed Pulao, Khichdi, warm Kadhi, and freshly puffed Puris or flat Rotis.

All items in the main Thali are unlimited, allowing you to enjoy your favorites to your heart's content. We finish the meal with traditional Indian desserts and a warm cup of cardamom-infused Masala Chai. Come hungry, leave completely satisfied.

Dine in Murray Hill, Manhattan

Vatan is proudly located on Third Avenue, in the Curry Hill / Murray Hill neighborhood of Manhattan, New York City. We are just a few blocks away from key Midtown landmarks. Easily accessible by the 6 Subway Train to the 28th Street Station. Simply walk three blocks East to Third Avenue.

If you are driving, metered street parking is readily available along Third Avenue and adjacent side streets. Additionally, there are several public parking garages located within one block of the restaurant for your convenience.
