Book Your Thali Feast Table

Due to the popular, all-you-can-eat nature of our Gujarati dining experience, we highly recommend making reservations in advance. Please read our reservation and cancellation policies below before booking.

Reservation Policies:

Our goal is to seat all guests in the quickest possible manner. Because of our busy seatings, we hold tables for a maximum of 10 minutes after your scheduled reservation time. If your group is delayed, please call us immediately at 1 (212) 689-5666.

Cancellation Policies:

To ensure fair availability to all diners, we require cancellations to be made at least 1 hour prior to your scheduled appointment. If canceled within 1 hour of the reservation time, a fee of 50% of the estimated charges will be applied.

In the event of a No Show without notice, a charge of 100% of the estimated charges will be billed to the card on file.

By clicking below, you agree to our Reservation and Cancellation Policies.
