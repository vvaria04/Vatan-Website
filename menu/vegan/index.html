Vegan Menu

Home » Menus » Vegan Menu

PRIX FIXE EXPERIENCE - $45

Note: Made strictly without dairy products or honey. Below is our complete menu tailored for vegan dining, offering a full assortment of dairy-free Indian vegetarian items.

Appetizer Thali

Batatavada

Deep-fried potato fritters seasoned with green chili, ginger, and coriander, coated in a light chickpea batter.

Ragda Patis

Savory potato cutlet served in a rich white bean sauce, seasoned with traditional spices.

Bhel

Puffed rice and Bengal gram mixed with sweet/spicy chutneys, onions, and coriander.

Samosa

Classic triangular crispy pastries filled with seasoned spicy potatoes and green peas.

Chana Masala

Savory black chickpeas slow-cooked with fresh tomatoes, onion, and spices.

Sev Puri (No Yogurt)

Garbanzo beans and sweet/spicy chutneys layered in a crispy puffed shell, served without dairy yogurt.

Mirchi Bhajia

Deep-fried hot peppers stuffed with proprietary spices and coated in batter.

Entree Thali

Bataka Nu Sak

Tender potatoes slow-cooked in a mild, savory red tomato gravy.

Moong

Whole green moong beans simmered in a mild, savory gravy.

Bhinda Nu Sak

Curried fresh okra cooked with cumin and traditional Gujarati spices.

Toor Dal

Yellow lentils cooked with mild spices, ginger, and lemon juice (no butter/ghee).

Chole

Classic chickpeas cooked in a spiced gravy with tomatoes and coriander.

Papadam

Crispy, thin roasted lentil wafers served as a crunchy accompaniment.

Vegetable of the Day

Special seasonal vegetable preparation created fresh by our chef daily.

Aam Ras

Sweet mango pulp puree (available upon request during seasons).

Puri / Roti

Freshly puffed deep-fried whole wheat bread (Puri) or soft whole wheat flatbread (Roti), prepared without ghee brushed on top.

Entree Complements

Pulao

Aromatic steamed basmati white rice cooked with green peas.

Khichdi

Comforting yellow lentils mixed with rice and assorted fresh vegetables (no ghee).

Dessert & Chai

Masala Chai

Aromatic hot black tea brewed with cardamom, ginger, and select spices (served without milk/cream).

Note: 18% admin fee (to cover operational cost) will be added. In compliance with our traditional dining style, we do not allow takeouts of any leftovers. Please inform your server of any food allergies prior to ordering. All menu items are subject to change according to seasonality and availability.
