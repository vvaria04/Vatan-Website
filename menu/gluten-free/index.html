POSSIBILITY OF CROSS CONTAMINATION: Please be advised that we do not have a separate kitchen for gluten-free foods. While we use a dedicated GF fryer and take strict care, there is a possibility of cross-contamination. 18% admin fee (to cover operational cost) will be added. No takeout of leftovers.
