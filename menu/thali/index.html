A Unique Table-Side Thali Experience in New York City

Unlike traditional self-service dining where you stand in long queues and serve yourself from communal chafing dishes, Vatan offers a premium table-side all-you-can-eat Thali meal. You relax at our custom hand-painted tables in a beautiful courtyard setting, and our attentive staff brings an endless assortment of fresh, piping hot delicacies directly to your table.

How It Works:

  1. Choose Your Base Dietary Thali: Choose from our Regular, Jain, Swaminarayan, Vegan, Gluten-Free, or Nut-Free options.
  2. The Appetizer Course: A massive tray consisting of Sev Puri, Ragda Patis, Samosas, Muthia, Batatavada, Mirchi Bhajia, Khaman, and Chana Masala is served to share.
  3. The Entree Course: A traditional round stainless-steel platter (Thali) containing separate bowls (Katoris) of Toor Dal, curried potatoes (Bataka Nu Sak), Bhaji (spinach & corn), Chole (chickpeas), seasonal vegetables, Kadhi, hot puffed Puris or flat Rotis, Pulao, Khichdi, and Papadam.
  4. Infinite Refills: Enjoy a dish? Simply ask your server for a refill. All items on the Prix Fixe Thali are unlimited.
  5. Dessert & Chai: Complete your royal meal with sweet Kheer (rice pudding), mango pulp (Aam Ras), homemade Indian ice cream, and a warm cup of authentic spiced Masala Chai.

Why Table-side Thali is Better:

  • ✔ Hygiene: No shared serving spoons or open food counters.
  • ✔ Freshness: Food is made hot in the kitchen and served immediately, retaining flavors.
  • ✔ Ambiance: Ideal for conversation without interruptions to stand up.
