Gluten-Free & Vegan Menu

Home » Menus » Gluten-Free & Vegan Menu

PRIX FIXE EXPERIENCE - $45

Note: Prepared strictly without wheat, rye, barley, oats, and dairy products. All fried items are cooked in a dedicated gluten-free fryer. Please note cross-contamination details below.

Appetizer Thali

Batatavada

Deep-fried savory potato dumplings coated in a gluten-free chickpea flour batter (dairy-free/onion-free/garlic-free).

Mirchi Bhajia

Fried hot pepper fritters seasoned with proprietary spices and coated in chickpea batter.

Bhel

Light puffed rice and Bengal gram mixed with sweet and spicy gluten-free/vegan chutneys.

Ragda Patis

Savory potato cutlet served in a rich white bean sauce, seasoned with traditional spices.

Chana Masala

Spiced black chickpeas cooked with fresh onions, tomatoes, and aromatic spices.

Entree Thali

Bataka Nu Sak

Tender potatoes slow-cooked in a mild, savory red tomato gravy.

Moong

Whole green moong beans simmered in a mild, savory gravy.

Bhinda Nu Sak

Curried fresh okra cooked with cumin and traditional Gujarati spices.

Toor Dal

Yellow lentils cooked with mild spices, ginger, and lemon juice (no butter/ghee).

Chole

Savory chickpeas cooked in a spiced gravy with tomatoes and coriander.

Papadam

Crispy, thin roasted lentil wafers served as a crunchy accompaniment.

Vegetable of the Day

Special seasonal vegetable preparation created fresh by our chef daily.

Aam Ras

Sweet mango pulp puree (Available upon request during seasons).

Rotla (Optional add-on) +$8

Traditional flatbread made from 100% black millet flour (1 piece).

Entree Complements

Pulao

Aromatic steamed basmati white rice cooked with green peas.

Khichdi

Comforting preparation of split moong dal lentils and rice (no ghee/dairy).

Dessert & Chai

Masala Chai

Aromatic hot black tea brewed with cardamom, ginger, and select spices (served without milk/cream).

POSSIBILITY OF CROSS CONTAMINATION: Please note that we do not have a separate kitchen for gluten-free items. While we use a dedicated GF fryer, cross-contamination is possible. 18% admin fee (to cover operational cost) will be added. No takeout of leftovers.
