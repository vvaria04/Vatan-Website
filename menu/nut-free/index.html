PRIX FIXE EXPERIENCE - $45

Note: All dishes prepared strictly without tree nuts or peanuts. Below is our complete menu tailored for nut-free safety and compliance.

Appetizer Thali

Batatavada

Deep-fried potato fritters seasoned with green chili and ginger, dipped in chickpea flour batter (no nuts).

Muthia

Steamed flour dumplings infused with fresh spinach and tempered mustard seeds.

Chana Masala

Savory garbanzo beans cooked with onion, tomato, cumin, and fresh coriander.

Ragda Patis (No Peanuts)

Potato cutlet served in a rich white bean sauce, prepared strictly peanut-free.

Khaman

Soft, steamed puffed cream-of-wheat flour cakes seasoned with herbs.

Samosa

Triangular crispy pastry shells filled with spicy potatoes and peas.

Mirchi Bhajia

Crispy fried hot pepper fritters dusted with tangy chaat spices.

Sev Puri

Garbanzo beans, fresh yogurt, and sweet/spicy chutneys layered in a crispy puffed shell.

Entree Thali

Bataka Nu Sak

Tender potatoes slow-cooked in a mild, savory red tomato gravy.

Moong

Whole green moong beans simmered in a mild, savory gravy.

Bhaji

Sauteed fresh spinach and sweet corn cooked with aromatic spices.

Papadam

Crispy, thin roasted lentil wafers served as a crunchy accompaniment.

Bhinda Nu Sak

Curried fresh okra cooked with cumin and traditional Gujarati spices.

Aam Ras

Sweet mango pulp puree (Available upon request during seasons).

Chole

Classic chickpeas cooked in a spiced gravy with tomatoes and coriander.

Puri / Roti

Freshly puffed deep-fried whole wheat bread (Puri) or soft whole wheat flatbread (Roti).

Vegetable of the Day

Special seasonal vegetable preparation created fresh by our chef daily.

Entree Complements

Khichdi

Comforting preparation of split yellow lentils and rice with spices.

Pulao

Aromatic steamed basmati white rice cooked with green peas.

Kadhi

Warm soup made with yogurt, chickpea flour, and tempered with authentic spices.

Dessert & Chai

Ice Cream

Rich, homemade traditional Indian ice cream (Kulfi flavour, nut-free version).

Masala Chai

Warm, aromatic Indian tea brewed with milk, cardamom, ginger, and select spices.

Dessert of the Day

Special dessert creation prepared daily to complement the Thali.

Note: 18% admin fee (to cover operational cost) will be added. In compliance with our traditional dining style, we do not allow takeouts of any leftovers.
