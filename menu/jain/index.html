PRIX FIXE EXPERIENCE - $45

Note: Prepared without Onion, Garlic, and Potatoes [not suitable for strict Jains]. Contains dairy. Below is the complete listing of our Jain compliant thali experience.

Appetizer Thali

Bhel

Puffed rice and Bengal gram mixed with a variety of sweet and spicy Jain-compliant sauces.

Mirchi Bhajia

Fried hot pepper fritters dusted with tangy chaat masala.

Chana Masala

Savory chickpeas cooked to perfection in aromatic spices and garnished with fresh coriander.

Ragdo

Warm, spiced white bean sauce seasoned with traditional herbs and cumin.

Khaman

Steamed puffed cream-of-wheat flour cakes seasoned with tempered mustard seeds.

Sev Puri

Garbanzo beans, fresh yogurt, and sweet/spicy chutneys layered in a crispy puffed shell.

Entree Thali

Bhinda Nu Sak

Curried fresh okra cooked with cumin and traditional Gujarati spices.

Papadam

Crispy, thin roasted lentil wafers served as a crunchy accompaniment.

Chole

Savory chickpeas slow-cooked with aromatic garam masala and tomato base.

Aam Ras

Sweet mango pulp puree (Available upon request during seasons).

Vegetable of the Day

Special seasonal vegetable preparation created fresh by our chef daily.

Kheer

Traditional creamy Indian rice pudding sweetened and garnished with dry fruits.

Moong

Whole green moong beans simmered in a mild, savory gravy.

Puri / Roti

Freshly puffed deep-fried whole wheat bread (Puri) or soft whole wheat flatbread (Roti).

Entree Complements

Pulao

Aromatic steamed basmati white rice cooked with green peas.

Kadhi

Warm soup made with yogurt, chickpea flour, and tempered with authentic spices.

Dessert & Chai

Ice Cream

Rich, homemade traditional Indian ice cream (Kulfi flavour).

Masala Chai

Warm, aromatic Indian tea brewed with milk, cardamom, ginger, and select spices.

Dessert of the Day

Special dessert creation prepared daily to complement the Thali.

Note: 18% admin fee (to cover operational cost) will be added. In compliance with our traditional dining style, we dxo not allow takeouts of any leftovers. Please inform your server of any food allergies prior to ordering. All menu items are subject to change according to seasonality and availability.
